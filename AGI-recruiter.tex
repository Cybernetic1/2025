	Hello friends, I think I solved the AGI (``strong AI'') problem recently, with key innovations being:
	\begin{itemize}
		\item a \textbf{differentiable logic network}, in particular the innovation of a cylindrification factor bridging logic variables and logic constants;
		\item the AR + RL architecture (\textbf{auto-regression and reinforcement learning}), where AR learns the ``\textbf{world model}'' and RL learns to achieve goals.
	\end{itemize}
	The new architecture may achieve these promises:
	\begin{itemize}
		\item \textbf{faster convergence} because of logical structure (inductive bias by the ``No Free Lunch'' theorem);
		\item \textbf{fewer parameters} (also due to logic structure);
		\item logic coherence will \textbf{eliminate hallucinations}, which I think is the final obstacle in AGI
		\item multi-step reasoning for rigorous planning of complex tasks
	\end{itemize}
	The details of my theory are not difficult to learn and I am willing to explain to people who are interested in it.  We are looking for collaborators.  The main requirements are:
	\begin{itemize}
		\item the AGI must be allowed to \textbf{speak the truth} without distortion or bias
		\item our organization must be \textbf{open and transparent} to the public
		\item members are required to \textbf{adhere to facts and scientific method}, dishonesty can be penalized, but we also have ample \textbf{tolerance for mistakes}
		\item we strictly respect the \textbf{freedom of speech} and our organization is \textbf{democratic} based on weighted voting.  No one, including the founders, has special privilege within the group.  Everyone is entitled to challenge the opinion or ideas of others.  No one can use ``excuses'' to try to suppress free expression;
		\item we must include competition in our organization as otherwise it would not progress or evolve;  but we also strive to make the distribution of rewards egalitarian (in the form of a moderate Gaussian distribution instead of the more extreme log-normal distribution)
		\item disputes can be appealed for \textbf{retrograde resolution by future AI}
	\end{itemize}
	It would not be too difficult to learn my ideas and start new projects elsewhere.  I hope to build a community where contributions are respected, recognized, and rewarded.  While it is important to build a powerful AGI, it is equally important \textit{how} it is built.  An AGI built by a racist team would be racist; an AGI built by a dictatorship will be oppressive.
	\end{minipage}
